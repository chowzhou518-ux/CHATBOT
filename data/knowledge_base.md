## Booking and Reservation Process

### How to Make a Reservation

1. **Required Information**:
   - Your first and last name
   - Your vehicle license plate number
   - Preferred space type
   - Start date and time
   - End date and time
   - Contact email or phone number

2. **Reservation Methods**:
   - Use this chatbot to provide your details
   - An administrator will review your request
   - You'll receive confirmation via email or phone
   - Present your confirmation at the entry gate

3. **Advance Booking**:
   - Reservations can be made up to 30 days in advance
   - Same-day reservations accepted subject to availability
   - Minimum reservation duration: 1 hour
   - Maximum reservation duration: 7 days

4. **Cancellation Policy**:
   - Free cancellation up to 2 hours before start time
   - 50% refund for cancellations within 2 hours of start time
   - No refund for no-shows

## Rules and Regulations

### Parking Rules
1. Park only within marked lines
2. Display your parking receipt visibly on dashboard
3. Do not block other vehicles or access lanes
4. Turn off engine while parked
5. Lock your vehicle; management not responsible for theft/damage
6. Obey all posted signs and directions

### Prohibited Activities
- Overnight parking without reservation (fee applies)
- Parking in reserved spaces without proper permit
- Vehicle washing or maintenance
- Storage of hazardous materials
- Abandoned vehicles (towed after 48 hours)

### EV Charging Station Rules
- 30-minute limit when others are waiting
- Move vehicle after charging complete
- Charging cables must be properly stored
- Report damaged equipment immediately
